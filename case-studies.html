Langenium

Consuming much of my spare time from 2012 to 2017, Langenium was a concept MMORPG built on socket.io, Three.JS and Node.JS. At its height the website received 1000 visits a month and had a working arcade style arena where players could anonymously hop into a flying vehicle and shoot one another.
