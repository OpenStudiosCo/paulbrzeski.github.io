Case studies

Throughout the course of my career most of my work has been confidental and this has not changed since switching to contracting. Unfortunately I am unable to disclose specifics about any of the work I've delivered in the past few years and the ones I can are too long ago to be relevant.

The time commitment, technical ability and innovative thinking I apply to my personal projects is comparable to my day to day work so I encourage you to review their case studies below.


Kamigen

Originally starting as a continuation of the Langenium project, Kamigen was meant to be a simple standalone aerial combat game based on the original elements of the online game. Unfortunately I got a bit carried away during the writing phase and ended up shifting gears to make a comic. Since then Kamigen has become entirely a multimedia project with any software development being purely in the service of producing media.

In its current form we are rebuilding the art using new techniques unlocked by Stable Diffusion.

Open Studios

Founded in 2019, Open Studios produces cutting edge digital media, software and high end goods. We operate a number of online stores, produce our own media IP and create fine jewellery and couture garments.

Open Studios is a tech leader and innovator, reducing the amount of labor required for production.

Langenium

Consuming much of my spare time from 2012 to 2017, Langenium was a concept MMORPG built on socket.io, Three.JS and Node.JS. At its height the website received 1000 visits a month and had a working arcade style arena where players could anonymously hop into a flying vehicle and shoot one another.

Work smarter, not harder.
